The problem

Most process maps are wall art.

They are drawn once for an audit, pinned to a wall, and never used to actually improve the flow.

Maps drawn once, never updated
No real cycle-time or wait data
Bottlenecks stay invisible
Waste is never quantified
Improvements are not prioritized
Gains are never tracked

So the value stream stays slow — and the waste stays hidden.

The solution

Flow Intelligence Copilot

A visual flow-mapping workspace with an AI operational-excellence advisor built in — taking teams from current-state map → metrics → bottleneck & waste analysis → prioritized improvements → future-state design.

Map the flow on a drag-and-drop canvas, capture the real metrics, and get an honest, evidence-based analysis — while keeping the human improvement owner in control.

Key capabilities

From a blank canvas to a prioritized improvement plan.

Visual Flow Canvas

Drag-and-drop value stream, process, metric-based, and future-state maps with 13 node types — suppliers, processes, queues, inspections, rework loops, kaizen bursts and more.

Metric-Based Mapping

Capture cycle time, wait time, WIP, first-pass yield, defect rate, changeover and uptime on every step — the data that real improvement actually needs.

AI Flow Analysis

An operational-excellence advisor finds bottlenecks, classifies the 8 wastes, flags process risks, and quantifies flow efficiency and rolled throughput yield.

Improvement Roadmap

Quick wins, kaizen opportunities, suggested experiments, and a 30/60/90-day roadmap — prioritized, with the questions to ask the process owner first.

Readiness & Performance Scores

Map-readiness, flow-performance, and improvement-confidence scores tell you how trustworthy the data is before you act on it — no over-claiming.

Import Existing Maps

Bring maps in from Miro, Lucid, CSV/JSON exports, or screenshots and PDFs — with AI extraction, a confidence score, and a human review step before anything loads.

Future-State Design

Recommendations for pull systems, FIFO lanes, pacemakers, and leveled scheduling — to design the target state, not just describe the current one.

Branded PDF Report

Export a professional, branded analysis report — ready for the leadership read-out and the improvement file.
