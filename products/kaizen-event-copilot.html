The problem

Most Kaizen events fail before they start.

They are poorly scoped, weakly prepared, inconsistently facilitated, and rarely sustained after the workshop.

Vague problem statement
Weak baseline data
Scope too broad
Wrong participants
Jumping to solutions too early
No sustainment plan

So events burn energy — and the gains quietly fade.

The solution

Kaizen Event Copilot

A structured Kaizen operating system that moves teams from problem → readiness → event design → root cause → countermeasures → action plan → sustainment → report-out.

It structures the event, surfaces gaps, strengthens problem-solving logic, and generates professional Kaizen outputs — while keeping the human facilitator in control.

Key capabilities

A copilot for the whole Kaizen lifecycle.

Kaizen Readiness Score

A 0–100 executive scorecard across 10 dimensions — with blockers, missing information, and go/no-go guidance before launch.

Charter & Agenda

Auto-generate a Kaizen charter, day-by-day agenda matched to event duration, pre-work checklist, and roles.

8-Waste Analysis

Classify observations into the eight wastes from your process context — evidence-based, never invented.

Root Cause Workbench

5-Why chains, Fishbone categories, and candidate root-cause hypotheses with evidence, confidence, and validation steps.

Countermeasures & Impact/Effort

Corrective/preventive options scored on impact and effort, grouped into quick wins, major projects, fill-ins, and low priority.

Action & Control Plans

Editable action plans with owners and due dates, plus a control plan of KPIs, frequencies, and reaction plans.

30/60/90 Sustainment

A built-in follow-up structure so the gains hold — adherence, benefit validation, and spread to other lines.

Export-Ready Report

A professional Kaizen report assembled from the event record — ready for the sponsor read-out and the quality file.
